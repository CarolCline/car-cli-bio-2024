Carolyn Cline - Pageants

Welcome to the official website of Carolyn Cline, the talented and beautiful Miss Florida and a multiple pageant winner. We are currently working on updating this site with more information about her biography and her pageant journey. Please check back soon for more exciting updates.

Carolyn has earned the titles for Miss New Mexico Universe 1973, First in Swimsuit for the Miss USA 1974 Pageant, Miss Florida 1978, Miss America 1979-2nd runner up, and she received the Talent Award for the Miss America 1979 Pageant.
