58th Annual Miss America Pageant

Carolyn Cline, a prominent figure in the 58th Annual Miss America Pageant TV Special, made a lasting impression as “Self - Miss Florida (2nd Runner-Up)” in the 1978 broadcast. This iconic TV event, which featured contestants from each state, was extended to a two-hour telecast, drawing a television audience of sixty million viewers. Every state titleholder, including Carolyn Cline, was guaranteed a minimum $1000 scholarship, and Miss America’s scholarship was raised to $10,000 . This significant recognition and achievement underscore Carolyn Cline’s notable presence and success in the pageant, contributing to the rich history of the Miss America competition. .
