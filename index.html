Welcome to the official website of Carolyn Cline

Carolyn Cline is a world-class performer who has dazzled audiences around the globe with her unique and exciting show. She has starred in Las Vegas, and, Hollywood, as well as in Tokyo, Bangkok, Manila, Papeete, and more.

With over thirty years of experience in the entertainment industry, Carolyn has shared the stage with legends like Bob Hope, Red Skeleton, Liberace, Tony Orlando, Lee Greenwood, and Les Elgartâ€™s Orchestra. She is a versatile and talented musician, singer, entertainer, and composer, who can play the piano, organ, guitar, banjo, mandolin, flute, violin, and drums. She holds a Bachelor of Music Degree and a Master of Arts Degree in Music Education.

Carolyn has earned several titles throughout her career, including Miss New Mexico Universe 1973, First in Swimsuit for the Miss USA 1974 Pageant, Miss Florida 1978, Miss America 1979-2nd runner up, and the Talent Award for the Miss America 1979 Pageant.

In addition to her own music, Carolyn also pays tribute to her late cousin, the iconic Patsy Cline, by singing her songs with her voice, phrasing, and style. Carolyn Cline brings the memories of Patsy Cline to life and captures the hearts of everyone who listens to her.
